(n.) a brand evolved with the intention to create exclusive streetwear

What we do

We collaborate with some of the most talented artists all around the world to bring their artworks through complex embroidery onto streetwear. The clothes become the canvas and the art is exhibited by you.

The little artworks carry powerful messages the artist wants to share with all of you. By wearing our clothes you wear their art. You become a patron of the arts or as it is called in German: Mazen.

ONE GUY, ONE VISION.

2021. The year the I (Fares) decided to embark on a journey: Building an art-based streetwear brand.

With our brand ricamowear we want to support aspiring artists and at the same time give them the opportunity to spread their art through our clothes.

At the center of the brand stands art. Art that inspires, affects, moves people and communicates to them through a universal language. Together with you, we want to create a culture of streetwear lovers and art enthusiasts and leverage this brand to empower talented artists and creatives.
